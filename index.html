Midwest Composites and What we Offer

Midwest Composites is a company in Wapakoneta, Ohio that provides services in the open-mold fiberglass, resin transfer molding, and upholstery industries. We pride ourselves on being able to produce high-quality products for automobiles, marine vehicles, and campers/RV's. We are able to take designs to mass produce in ranges of large, bulk orders to small orders. We have been able to partner and work with companies such as Airstream and Cadillac to produce products ranging from benches and bucket seats to fiberglass doors and window shades.

While we are able to mass produce for our partnerships, we are able to take on individual orders! We can work with you to draft and create custom or follow OEM designs to help individuals restore or customize their products. If you're interested, contact us or stop on by to see our showcase of products that we have worked on to show what we can offer you!
