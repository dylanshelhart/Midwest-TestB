Who is Midwest Composites?

Midwest Composites is a company that was established in 1994, in Wapakoneta, Ohio. Today, Midwest Composites still proudly located in the same location since the orginal building was built. We have updated and upgraded so that we can provide services in the open-mold fiberglass, resin transfer molding, and upholstery industries and keep up with the everchanging industry standards. We pride ourselves on being able to produce high-quality products for automobiles, marine vehicles, and campers/RV's. We are able to take designs to mass produce in ranges of large, bulk orders to small orders. We have been able to partner and work with companies such as Airstream and Cadillac to produce products ranging from benches and bucket seats to fiberglass doors and window shades.

While we are able to mass produce for our partnerships, we are able to take on individual orders! We can work with you to draft and create custom or follow OEM designs to help individuals restore or customize their products. If you're interested, contact us or stop on by to see our showcase of products that we have worked on to show what we can offer you!

While we are work with major corporations and local customers alike, we take pride in where we are established. We host and support local community events, participating and giving back to the community that supports us, as much as we can. Some of the ways that we give back to our community is by hosting food drives, sponsoring Wapakoneta High School Athletic events, sponsoring local collegiate events, and donating to local charities. We take pride in being able to hire a diverse workforce and welcome all local talent to join our family to help us reach the next level in our potential. If you're interested in joining the Midwest Composites' family, stop by to check available positions and leave a resume, or submit your resume for your interested open positions!
