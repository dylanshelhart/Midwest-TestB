Midwest Composites' Portfolio

Midwest Composites prides itself on being able to help, work for, and partner with a large varieties of individuals. Some of our partnerships and previous works include working with companines and indivduals such as: Airstream, Cadillac, Wapakoneta Athletics, Wapakoneta Fire Department, Police, and EMS, Rhodes State College, the Ohio State University (Lima Branch), and many more!

See the work that we've done:

Airstream

One of our proud partners would be none other than Airstream! Airstream has been partnered with Midwest Composites since 2006! We produce doors, benches, chairs, and more for Airstream trailers.

Cadillac

Another great partner of Midwest Composites has been Cadillac. Partnered in 2013, We have been building rear bench seats and front bucket seats.

Foam and More Logistics

Foam and More Logisitics has been supplying Midwest Composites with materials since the business was started back in 1994.

Wilson Technologies

Wilson Technologies has been partnered with Midwest Composites since 2002 and has been our go to provider when it comes to equipment and hardware.
